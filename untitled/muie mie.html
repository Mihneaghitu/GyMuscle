What is Football Fantasy 2017?

Football Fantasy offers you a complete list of all players from the most importants leagues around the globe. It is supposed to give you information about players and teams while also giving you the opportunity to create your own dream team. Your accounts starts off with 10.000.000$ in the wallet, connecting everyday gives you a bonus of 5.000.000$ per day (participating in events also gives you money). You can use the money on the Transfer Market to buy new players. Of course, players have diffrent values, a stronger player will be more expensive than a not so good one, just connect daily to rack up that money and buy that favourite player of yours.
